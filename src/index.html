Understanding Punishment Windows

In VF5 REVO, punishment is crucial. Here's a breakdown of common punishment scenarios:

  • -10 to -12 frames: Jab punishment (P or 2P)
  • -13 to -14 frames: Mid punch combos (PP, 6P+K)
  • -15 to -17 frames: Strong mids (3P+K, 6P)
  • -18+ frames: Launcher punishment opportunities

Training Scenarios

Throw Escapes

Practice your throw escape timing. In VF5 REVO, you have approximately 10 frames to input a throw escape.

  • P+G: Escapes forward throws
  • P+G (neutral): Escapes neutral throws
  • Direction + P+G: Escapes directional throws

Evade Practice

Master the evade system (E button). Successful evades grant massive frame advantage.

  • Evade high attacks
  • Evade linear moves
  • Counter with evade attacks

Defensive Options

Practice your defensive toolkit:

  • Guard Impact (4P+K+G)
  • Sabaki moves (character-specific)
  • Reversal timing
  • Low punch fuzzy guard

Game Mechanics Overview

Triangle System

The core of VF's gameplay:

  • Strikes beat Throws
  • Throws beat Guards
  • Guards beat Strikes

Frame Advantage

Understanding frame data is crucial:

  • Plus frames = Your turn
  • Minus frames = Opponent's turn
  • Calculate advantage after every move

Stance Types

Different attack heights:

  • High: Can be ducked
  • Mid: Must be blocked or evaded
  • Low: Must be low blocked
  • Special Mid: Cannot be evaded

Counter Hit System

Hitting during opponent's attack:

  • Normal counter: +2 frames
  • Major counter: Extended juggle
  • Crumple stun states
